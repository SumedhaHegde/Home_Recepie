Dish Discovery

Breakfast

Dosa

To make dosa, first, prepare a batter by blending 1 cup of rice and 1/2 cup of urad dal with water, ferment it overnight. Heat a non-stick griddle, pour a ladle of batter, and spread it into a thin circle. Cook until golden brown, and serve with chutney and sambar for a delicious South Indian meal.

Palav

To make a delicious vegetable pulao, start by saut√©ing onions, garlic, and spices in a pot. Add washed rice, mixed vegetables, and water. Simmer until the rice is cooked and garnish with fresh coriander. Enjoy your flavorful and fragrant vegetable pulao!

Poori

To make delicious pooris, mix 2 cups of whole wheat flour with a pinch of salt and water to form a stiff dough. Divide the dough into small balls, roll them into thin rounds, and deep-fry in hot oil until they puff up and turn golden brown. Serve hot with your favorite curry or chutney.

Idli

Idli is a popular South Indian dish made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. To prepare idli, soak equal parts of rice and urad dal, grind them into a smooth batter, and let it ferment for about 8-12 hours. Then, steam the batter in idli molds to create soft, fluffy, and delicious idlis, typically served with chutney.

Lunch

South Indian

A traditional South Indian meal, known as a "thali," typically consists of rice served with a variety of flavorful and aromatic vegetarian dishes, such as sambar, rasam, curries, chutneys, and papad. It often includes a sweet treat like payasam or a banana for dessert, providing a harmonious blend of tastes and textures.

North Indian

A North Indian meal consists of aromatic dishes like roti (Indian bread) or rice served with various curries, including dal (lentil soup), sabzi (vegetable curry), and often accompanied by yogurt, pickles, and chutney, providing a balanced blend of flavors and textures. Spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala are used generously to create rich, flavorful dishes

Italian

A typical Italian meal typically consists of multiple courses, starting with antipasti (appetizers), followed by a primo (pasta or rice dish), a secondo (main course, often meat or fish), and concluding with dolce (dessert). Meals are often accompanied by bread and wine.

Mexican

A Mexican meal consists of a combination of dishes such as tacos, enchiladas, or tamales, accompanied by sides like rice and beans. Additional elements often include salsa, guacamole, and fresh tortillas. Mexican cuisine is renowned for its vibrant flavors and ingredients like chilies, cilantro, and lime.

Chats

Pani puri

To make pani puri, prepare a spicy, tangy pani (water) by mixing tamarind chutney, mint-coriander chutney, and spices. Fill hollow puris with a mixture of mashed potatoes, chickpeas, and onions, and then dunk them into the pani for a delightful, bite-sized snack. Enjoy immediately for the best taste!

Samosa

To make samosas, prepare a spiced potato and pea filling. Wrap it in triangular pastry sheets, and deep-fry until golden brown. Serve these crispy, savory pastries with chutney for a delicious snack or appetizer

Pavbaji

To make pav bhaji, cook a medley of mashed vegetables with a fragrant spice blend. Serve this flavorful mixture with buttered pav (bread rolls) and garnish with chopped onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lemon juice for a popular Indian street food delight.

Vadapav

For vada pav, make spiced potato fritters by mashing boiled potatoes with spices. Place the vada in a pav (soft bread roll) and add chutney. Serve this popular Indian street food with fried green chilies.

Desert

Pavlova

To prepare pavlova, whisk egg whites until stiff, gradually adding sugar and vinegar. Shape the mixture into a nest on a baking sheet, bake until crisp on the outside, and soft inside. Fill the pavlova with whipped cream and fresh fruit for a delightful dessert.

Tiramisu

To create tiramisu, blend mascarpone cheese with sugar and egg yolks until creamy. Soak ladyfingers in espresso and layer them with the mascarpone mixture. Dust with cocoa powder, refrigerate, and serve for a classic Italian dessert.

Brownie

To make brownies, melt chocolate and butter, then mix with sugar and eggs. Add flour and cocoa powder, bake in a greased pan, and cool before cutting into squares. Enjoy these rich and fudgy chocolate treats!

Cheesecake

To make cheesecake, blend cream cheese, sugar, and eggs until smooth. Pour the mixture over a graham cracker crust and bake. Chill, then top with fruit, chocolate, or caramel for a delectable dessert.
